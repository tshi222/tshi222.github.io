Context

This map displays each street segment in Fayette County, color-coded to the total number of car-on-pedestrian and car-on-bicycle collisions recorded in the period between Jan. 2004 and Apr. 2016. Clicking on a segment shows the breakdown of collisions in the segment. Note the high density of collisions in the downtown area and along the arterial streets such as Nicholasville.

Data Sources

Collision data was collected from http://crashinformationky.org/ and street segment data was collected from http://data.lexingtonky.gov/ . The map was produced on the CartoDB platform.
